Small Business Web Design

1

1st Things 1st

Initial meeting with client to discuss scope of the project.

2

Strategy

Personalized strategy document drafted based on client's goals, target audience and improvements or scope of design a new or existing website needs in order to be successful.

3

Information Architecture

A new improved user experience document is created, documenting all elements in preparationg for the design and development stages.

4

Design Mockups

With approval of the website architecture, a wireframe will be created. This is essentially a blueprint of the website outlining the structure. After wireframes are designed, colors, logos as well as branding elements will be added to create a static design of the website.

5

Coding & Development

After the approval of designs, they move into the development stage where the static site is being converted into an interactive working website.

6

Testing

Before the website is going live, it's being tested to ensure that all functions is working properly and the design also matches the original client-approved designs.

7

Launching

The new website is being pushed live to the public. Performance is monitored and optimized whenever possible to maximize results.
